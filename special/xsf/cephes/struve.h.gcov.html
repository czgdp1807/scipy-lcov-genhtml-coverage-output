      26             :  *

      29             :  * The Bessel function expansion tends to fail for |z| >~ |v| and is not tried
      30             :  * there.
      31             :  *

      33             :  * produce reasonable results.
      34             :  *
      35             :  * (iii)

      37             :  * The three expansions together cover for Struve H the region z > 0, v real.
      38             :  *
      39             :  * They also cover Struve L, except that some loss of precision may occur around
      40             :  * the transition region z ~ 0.7 |v|, v < 0, |v| >> 1 where the function changes
      41             :  * rapidly.
      42             :  *
